Academic Profile

Milad Badeleh

Senior Researcher

Milad Badeleh

Senior Research Fellow at Oxford Quantum Institute

I am a passionate researcher specializing in quantum computing and its applications in machine learning. My work focuses on developing novel quantum algorithms and optimizing quantum error correction techniques to push the boundaries of what's computationally possible.

Currently, I lead the Quantum Machine Learning research group at Oxford University, where we explore the intersection of quantum physics and artificial intelligence to solve complex problems in optimization, pattern recognition, and data analysis.

Institution: University of Oxford
Department: Quantum Computing Institute
Research Focus: Quantum Machine Learning
Position: Senior Research Fellow
