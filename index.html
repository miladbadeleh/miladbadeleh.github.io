Masters Student at Science and Research Branch of IAU University

I am a passionate researcher specializing in Machine Learning and Computer Vision and its applications. My work focuses on developing novel algorithms and optimizing error correction techniques to push the boundaries of what's computationally possible.

Currently, I lead the Machine Learning research group at IAU University, where we explore the intersection of robotics and artificial intelligence to solve complex problems in optimization, pattern recognition, and data analysis.
